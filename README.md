|-----------------|-----------------------------------------|----------------|---------|
|song             |Top level                                |song data object|yes      |
|&nbsp; title     |Song title                               |string          |yes      |
|&nbsp; artist    |Song artist                              |string          |yes      |
|&nbsp; musicians |A list of musicians who play on the song |array of strings|no       |
